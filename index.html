01 — Mono no Aware

The Soul
of Spring

Sakura — cherry blossoms — paint Japan pink for only one fleeting week. Their power lies in their brevity. This is mono no aware: the tender ache of beautiful things passing. Anime captures it perfectly — petals on a rooftop, a first meeting, a goodbye.
